Brisket

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds first-cut brisket (or flat-cut), trimmed of fat
  • 4 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large yellow onion, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh rosemary
  • 1 cup apple juice

Instructions

  1. Season both sides of the brisket generously with salt, pepper, and cayenne. place brisket over an aluminum foil and refrigerate, uncovered, 8 to 12 hours.
  2. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C)
  3. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and salt. Cook and stir for about 5 minutes. Stir in garlic, rosemary, and apple juice
  4. Transfer half of the apple-onion mixture into a baking dish. Place brisket fat-side up on top; cover with remaining apple-onion mixture. Cover baking dish tightly with foil.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour 30 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 250 degrees F and bake for about 2 hours 15 minutes. Check for doneness after 2 hours.
  6. Transfer brisket to a plate and tent with foil.
  7. Pour the onions and braising liquid into a large measuring cup or bowl. Skim some fat off the top. Puree with a stick blender to create a gravy.
  8. Slice brisket across the grain and serve with the gravy.
